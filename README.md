# neighbro.place
NEIGHBRO — the global face of a hyperlocal neighbor network. Meet people in your building, office, or block by matching on shared vibes and interests, not endless swiping. When two neighbors click, a light live chat opens, then it's up to you to take it further. Frontend only — one of two faces sharing a single backend.
